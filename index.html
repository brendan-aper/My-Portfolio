About me

Hey there, I'm Brendan! I'm a contractor and the owner of my own tile company based in beautiful Bend, Oregon. I've been doing this for over a decade now, and I love every minute of it. But before I got into construction, I was really into computer science. That's why I decided to enroll in a programming bootcamp, and it's been a blast!


When I'm not working, I'm usually playing guitar or watching movies with my girlfriend Leanna. We've been together for 5 years now, and we're both really into cinema. In fact, we try to watch at least one new movie every week! Oh, and we can't forget about our cat Bubby who's always there to keep us company


I take a lot of pride in my work, and I always strive to provide the best service and craftsmanship possible. It's been an amazing journey building my own business and working with all sorts of clients over the years, and I'm excited to see what the future holds!
